Artifacts

These are time-tested assignments to challenge the students to use varied resources to create traditional programming "artifacts."

They may not appear striking or stunning, but they depict efforts of developing a muscle for independent learning and technological prowess.

I would have had a hard time completing these without the efforts of those who came before me.

This artifact involved embedding information in a resume. I used HTML for this simple first assignment.
